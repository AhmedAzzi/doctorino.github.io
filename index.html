Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How secure is my medical data on Doctorino?

Doctorino employs enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data centers, and strict access controls. We are fully HIPAA compliant and regularly undergo security audits to ensure your medical information remains private and protected.

Can I use Doctorino with my existing healthcare provider?

Yes! Doctorino is designed to work with your existing healthcare providers. You can invite your doctors to join the platform, or if they're already using Doctorino, you can connect with them directly. Our system also allows for secure sharing of medical records with providers who don't use our platform.

How do telemedicine consultations work?

Telemedicine consultations on Doctorino are simple and convenient. You can schedule an appointment through the app, receive a reminder before your consultation, and join a secure video call with your healthcare provider at the scheduled time. After the consultation, any prescriptions or follow-up instructions will be available in your account.

Is Doctorino available on all devices?

Doctorino is available on multiple platforms. Healthcare providers typically use our desktop application, while patients can access their information through our mobile apps available for both iOS and Android devices. We also offer a web version that works on any modern browser.

How does the AI-powered diagnostic system work?

Our AI diagnostic tools analyze medical images and data using advanced machine learning algorithms trained on millions of validated cases. The system can identify patterns and anomalies in ECGs, mammograms, and other medical imaging to assist healthcare providers in making more accurate diagnoses. It's important to note that our AI tools are designed to support, not replace, the expertise of medical professionals.

What does Doctorino cost?

Doctorino offers several pricing tiers to meet different needs. For patients, we have a free basic plan that includes appointment scheduling and medical record storage. Premium patient plans include additional features like telemedicine consultations and medication management. For healthcare providers, we offer subscription plans based on practice size and required features. Contact our sales team for detailed pricing information.
